Greedy Crocodiles

Greedy Crocodiles is a deflationary collection of 555 unique NFTs on the Solana Blockchain

Lore

It was a shiny day in the world of Greedy Crocodiles.

For days they haven't seen any fish in the nearby river and so they are hungry. They decided to ask the king, because he is the best fisherman among the Greedy Crocodiles and can swim to other rivers to look for food.

The king was successful. He managed to find 200 fish, which will be distributed among all Greedy Crocodiles.

Oh no! Suddenly a tornado appeared. Which Greedy Crocodiles will he carry away?


The tornado will buy the floor and burn it.
There will be a 200 unique fish NFTs airdrop after the mint, of which 100 NFTs will be randomly distributed among all Greedy Crocodiles holder and the other 100 will be distributed among OG members.
